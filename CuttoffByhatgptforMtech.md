📌 Understanding the Difference Between Cutoffs
There are two types of cutoffs:
✔ Qualifying Cutoff (Basic Eligibility)
This is the minimum score you must get to appear in counselling — generally low (e.g., CS ~29.2 marks scaled to ~700 score ≈ cutoff eligibility). Collegedunia
✔ Admission Cutoff
This is the actual score required to get admitted in a specific department at an IIT after counselling — much higher. What we listed above are admission cutoffs, not just qualifying marks. Careers360

📍 How to Find Exact & Official Cutoffs
After GATE results and COAP counselling rounds (usually May–June):
🔹 Each IIT publishes its closing & opening score for each M.Tech program
🔹 This will include exact numbers for CS, Data Science, AI, Computational, etc.
You’ll find them on:
✅ COAP official portal
✅ Individual IIT admission pages
✅ Official CCMT cutoff reports ccmt.admissions.nic.in
